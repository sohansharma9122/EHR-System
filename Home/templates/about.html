Introduction to EHR

An Electronic Health Record (EHR) system is a digital platform that stores, manages, and secures patient health information. Our EHR system is designed to streamline healthcare processes, making patient data easily accessible for authorized users while ensuring privacy and security.

Purpose / Goals

  • Provide a centralized system for managing patient records, appointments, and prescriptions.
  • Improve efficiency for doctors, staff, and patients.
  • Generate bills, lab reports, and prescriptions automatically.
  • Allow easy monitoring and management of hospital workflows.

Key Features by Role

Admin
  • Manage profile and view all user data
  • Edit or delete records
  • Generate bills
Doctor
  • View and update patient details
  • Approve or decline appointments
  • Add prescriptions
Patient
  • Manage profile
  • View doctor information
  • Book and check appointments
  • View bills
Staff
  • Manage profile
  • View doctor and patient details
  • Add lab tests and prices
  • Generate lab test reports
